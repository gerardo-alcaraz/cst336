Passwords

NEVER do this with your passwords.

  • Don't use easily guessed passwords like "123456" or "asdf123"
  • Don't put personal information such as name, hometown, pet name, etc.
  • Don't use words that are available in dictionaries, this leaves you vulnerable to dictionary attacks.
  • Don't use the same password for different websites. Especially your password for your email.
  • If your password is compromised the attackers will have access to your email as well.
